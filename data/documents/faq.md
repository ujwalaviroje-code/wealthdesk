# BNB Frequently Asked Questions

## Account and General Banking

**What types of accounts does BNB offer?**
BNB offers savings accounts (for individuals, interest-bearing), current accounts (for businesses with higher transaction volumes, no interest), and salary accounts (zero-balance accounts opened through an employer for salaried employees). For account opening details, visit any BNB branch or complete Video KYC on the BNB mobile app.

**How do I find the IFSC code for my BNB branch?**
Your IFSC code is printed on your cheque book and appears on your bank statement. You can also find it on the BNB website under the Branch Locator section by searching for your branch name or city.

**What are BNB's customer care hours?**
The toll-free number (1800-103-1906) is available from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday to Saturday. For ATM-related emergencies (card blocked, suspicious transaction), a 24-hour helpline is available -- the number is printed on the back of your debit card.

**How do I open a new savings account?**
Visit any BNB branch with your Aadhaar, PAN, and a passport-sized photograph. Alternatively, complete the Video KYC process on the BNB mobile app to open a basic savings account digitally.

**How do I transfer money to another bank?**
Use NEFT, RTGS, or IMPS through internet banking or the mobile app. IMPS is available 24x7 including weekends and holidays. NEFT operates in half-hourly batches. RTGS is for amounts above Rs. 2 lakhs and is available during bank working hours.

## Loans

**How do I check the status of my loan application?**
Log in to the BNB mobile app and go to Loans > Application Status. Alternatively, call customer care with your application reference number.

**Can I apply for a loan if I already have an existing BNB loan?**
Yes, as long as your combined EMI obligations (existing plus new) remain within the eligibility threshold for your income level. The credit assessment will evaluate your total debt-service ratio.

**What happens if I want to switch from one loan type to another?**
BNB does not offer a direct loan type conversion. You would need to close the existing loan and apply fresh for the new product. For example, closing a personal loan and applying for a gold loan are separate transactions.

**How long does it take for a home loan to be approved and disbursed?**
There are two stages. First, after your application is submitted, BNB conducts a credit appraisal and issues a conditional sanction letter within 10 working days of approval. Second, after sanction, document verification and legal clearance must be completed. For ready-to-move properties, disbursement to the seller typically takes a further 5 to 10 working days. For under-construction properties, disbursement follows construction milestones and has no fixed timeline.

**Can a Non-Resident Indian (NRI) take a home loan from BNB?**
BNB offers NRI home loans. The eligibility criteria and documentation differ from resident Indian home loans. Contact the NRI Banking desk at any metro BNB branch for details.

**Can I take a home loan for a property I already own?**
A loan against an existing property (LAP) is a separate product. A home loan is for purchasing or constructing a new property.

**What documents do I need to apply for a BNB home loan?**
To apply for a BNB home loan you need three types of documents. (1) Identity and address proof: PAN card and Aadhaar card for all applicants. (2) Income proof: salaried applicants must submit last 3 months' salary slips, last 6 months' bank statement, and Form 16 or ITR for the last 2 years; self-employed applicants must submit last 2 years' ITR with P&L statements and last 12 months' business bank statement. (3) Property documents: sale deed or agreement to sell, approved building plan, NOC from builder or society, and property tax receipts.

**Is the home loan interest tax deductible?**
Under Section 24(b) of the Income Tax Act, the interest portion of the EMI on a home loan is tax deductible. Consult your tax advisor for the applicable limit.

**Can I change from floating to fixed rate?**
BNB does not currently offer a mid-tenure switch from floating to fixed rate. New fixed-rate home loan products are available on application.

## Fixed Deposits

**Does the BNB 5-year FD qualify for tax deduction under Section 80C?**
Yes. BNB's 5-year Tax Saving Fixed Deposit qualifies for a deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, up to Rs. 1.5 lakhs per financial year. The 5-year lock-in cannot be broken early, and no loan can be taken against a Tax Saving FD. This is a product feature, not personalised tax advice.

**Can I open an FD online?**
Yes. Log in to internet banking or the BNB mobile app, go to Deposits > Open Fixed Deposit, choose your tenure and payout option, and confirm with your transaction OTP.

**What happens to my FD if I do not renew it at maturity?**
The amount sits in your account as a savings balance and earns savings account interest (not FD interest) until you instruct the bank. It is better to either auto-renew or convert it into a new FD immediately.

**Can I have multiple FDs in different names in my family?**
Yes. Each individual's FDs are counted separately for DICGC insurance (Rs. 5 lakhs per depositor per bank) and for TDS calculation purposes.

**Can I use an FD as collateral for a credit card?**
BNB offers a secured credit card against an FD as collateral. Credit card products are not covered in detail by WealthDesk -- visit any BNB branch for current product availability, credit limit terms, and the application process.

## Digital Banking

**I forgot my internet banking password. How do I reset it?**
Go to bnb.co.in/ibank and click on "Forgot Password." Verify with your registered mobile number OTP and account details, then set a new password.

**The BNB mobile app is not loading. What should I do?**
Check whether your phone is running the latest version of the app. Force-stop the app, clear the cache (Android: Settings > Apps > BNB > Storage > Clear Cache), and restart. If the issue persists, call technical support or visit a branch.

**Is there a transaction limit on IMPS?**
IMPS transfer limits vary by account type and registration status. The standard limit for retail customers is up to Rs. 5 lakhs per transaction. You can request a limit enhancement through the mobile app under Settings > Transaction Limits.

## WealthDesk and AI Services

**What is WealthDesk?**
WealthDesk is BNB's AI banking assistant. It answers questions about home loans, personal loans, car loans, fixed deposits, branch locations, and general BNB policies. It cannot access your account records or provide personalised investment advice.

**Can WealthDesk access my account details?**
No. WealthDesk cannot view your account balance, transaction history, or personal records. For account-specific queries, use internet banking, the mobile app, or contact customer care.

**Can WealthDesk give me investment advice?**
WealthDesk provides general information about BNB's savings and investment products. It cannot provide personalised financial or investment advice. For a formal suitability assessment, speak with a BNB Relationship Manager.

**What should I do if WealthDesk gives me incorrect information?**
WealthDesk strives for accuracy but may occasionally be incorrect, especially if rates or policies have changed recently. Always verify key product details (current interest rates, fees, eligibility criteria) with a BNB branch or the official BNB website before making a financial decision.
