# BNB Customer Policies

## Privacy and Data Protection

Bharat National Bank processes your personal and financial data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). The key principles that govern how BNB handles your data are:

**Purpose limitation:** Data collected for one purpose (e.g., processing a loan application) is not used for a different unrelated purpose without your consent.

**Data minimisation:** BNB collects only the data that is necessary for the service you have requested. You will never be asked for data that has no bearing on your transaction.

**Storage limitation:** BNB retains your data for the period required by applicable law and internal policy. Data that is no longer needed is securely deleted.

**Right to erasure:** You can request deletion of your personal data where there is no legal obligation for BNB to retain it. Submit a request through the BNB Data Privacy Portal or write to the Data Protection Officer.

**Consent:** For marketing communications, BNB requires explicit consent. You can withdraw consent for marketing at any time through the mobile app settings or by calling customer care.

BNB does not share your personal or financial data with any third party except as required by law (RBI, SEBI, income tax authorities) or for services directly related to your banking transaction (credit bureaus, KYC utilities).

## Customer Grievance Redressal

BNB follows a three-tier grievance mechanism:

**Tier 1 -- Branch and Digital Channels:**
Raise a complaint at the branch, via the BNB mobile app, or by calling 1800-103-1906 (toll-free, 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday to Saturday). The target resolution time for Tier 1 complaints is 10 working days.

**Tier 2 -- Nodal Officer:**
If the Tier 1 resolution is unsatisfactory, escalate to the Regional Nodal Officer. Contact details are available at every BNB branch and on the website. Target resolution time: 15 working days.

**Tier 3 -- Banking Ombudsman:**
If you remain unsatisfied after exhausting BNB's internal process, you can approach the Banking Ombudsman under the RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme. This is a free service. Details at rbi.org.in.

## Investment Product Compliance (SEBI Guidelines)

BNB distributes mutual funds and insurance products in partnership with SEBI-registered entities. For any investment product discussion, the following disclosures apply:

- Past performance does not guarantee future returns.
- Mutual fund investments are subject to market risk. Please read the scheme information document carefully before investing.
- BNB acts as a distributor, not an investment advisor. Personalised investment advice should be sought from a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser (IA).

WealthDesk, the AI banking assistant, can provide general information about BNB's financial products. It cannot provide personalised investment advice or a formal suitability assessment. For investment recommendations tailored to your specific financial situation, please speak with a BNB Relationship Manager or a registered Investment Adviser.

## Fair Lending Practice

BNB is committed to non-discriminatory lending. Loan decisions are based on creditworthiness criteria (income, credit score, repayment history, employment stability) that apply equally to all applicants. Factors such as religion, caste, gender, or regional origin play no part in credit decisions and are not considered in the credit appraisal process.

## KYC (Know Your Customer) Requirements

All BNB customers must complete KYC. For new accounts and loan applications, the standard KYC documents are a government-issued photo identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or voter ID) and an address proof. Existing customers must complete periodic KYC updates as mandated by RBI guidelines. Failure to complete a KYC update may result in temporary restrictions on certain account transactions.

## Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Policy

BNB is required by the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002 (PMLA) to monitor transactions for suspicious activity and report to the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU). Large cash transactions and unusual transaction patterns may trigger a verification request from the bank. Customers are required to cooperate with such requests. This is a legal obligation and is not specific to any individual customer profile.

## Security and Fraud Prevention

**BNB will never ask for your PIN, OTP, or password.** Any call, SMS, or email requesting this information is a fraud attempt. Disconnect immediately and report it to 1800-103-1906.

You are responsible for keeping your internet banking credentials and debit/credit card details secure. Do not share credentials with family members, bank employees, or third-party service providers.

If you suspect fraud on your account, freeze your account immediately through the BNB mobile app (Settings > Account Security > Freeze Account) and call customer care. BNB has a zero-liability policy for fraud reported within 3 days of the fraudulent transaction, subject to the investigation outcome.
